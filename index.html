HANSHENG XUE | 薛寒生

I am a Research Assistant at National University of Singapore (NUS), supervised by Vaibhav Rajan. I submitted my doctoral thesis and was a Ph.D. student in School of Computing at The Australian National University (ANU) in 2023. My Ph.D. supervisors are Prof. Yu Lin, Vaibhav Rajan, and Lexing Xie. Before coming to ANU, I obtained my M.Eng. degree in computer science at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Yadong Wang and Jiajie Peng. I earned my B.M. degree from Nanjing Tech University (NJTech) in 2015. I also had the opportunity to visit or intern at NPU, Fudan, NUS, BioMap, and Alibaba Group during my academic pursuits.

I have a broad interest in both Machine Learning and Computational Biology. In particular, I work on representation learning on graphs, deep graph clustering, combinatorial optimization and their applications in computational genomics, functional genomics, and multi-omics.

  1. Graph Neural Networks: Temporal, Bipartite, and Multiplex/Multi-View Networks;
  2. Computational Genomics: Metagenomic Binning, Haplotype Phasing, and Function Prediction.
