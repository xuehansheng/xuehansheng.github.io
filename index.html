HANSHENG XUE | 薛寒生

I am a Research Assistant at National University of Singapore (NUS), supervised by Vaibhav Rajan. I submitted my doctoral thesis in Oct. 2023 and was a PhD student in School of Computing at Australian National University (ANU). My PhD supervisors are Prof. Yu Lin, Vaibhav Rajan (NUS), and Lexing Xie. I also had the opportunity to visit or intern at Fudan, NUS, BioMap, and Alibaba Group during my PhD studies.

My research interests span Machine Learning and Computational Biology, focusing on representation learning on graphs, combinatorial optimization, and their practical applications in computational genomics, systems biology, and healthcare.

  1. Graph Neural Networks: Temporal, Bipartite, and Multiplex/Multi-View Networks;
  2. Computational Genomics: Metagenomic Contigs Binning, and Haplotype Phasing;
  3. Healthcare: Drug Reponse Prediction, Function Prediction, and Disease Diagnosis.

For more details, please see my CV. You can also find me on Linkedin and GitHub.
