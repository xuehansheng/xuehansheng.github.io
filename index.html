About Me

I currently a first second-year Ph.D student in the Research School of Computer Science at The Australian National University, Canberra, Australia. My advisor is Prof. Yu Lin. Before ANU, I obtained my M.Eng degree in computer science at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Yadong Wang. Besides, I ever cooperated with Assoc. Prof. Jiajie Peng (Northwestern Polytechnical University, China) from 2015 to 2019. I earned my B.M. degree from Nanjing University of Technology (NJUT) in 2015. I have a broad interest in both the Machine Learning and Computational Biology. In particular, I work on graph representation learning, un/self-supervised learning and their applications in metagenomics, functional genomics and proteomics.
