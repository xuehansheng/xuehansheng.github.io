Hansheng Xue | 薛寒生

I am a first second third final-year Ph.D. student in School of Computing at The Australian National University, Canberra, Australia. My supervisors are Prof. Yu Lin, Lexing Xie, and Vaibhav Rajan (NUS). Before coming to ANU, I obtained my M.Eng degree in computer science at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Yadong Wang and Jiajie Peng. I earned my B.M. degree from Nanjing Tech University (NJTech) in 2015. Besides, I also spent some time at Northwestern Polytech. Univ., Fudan Univ., Alibaba Group, and BioMap during my academic study.

I have a broad interest in both Machine Learning and Computational Biology. In particular, I work on representation learning on graphs, deep graph clustering, combinatorial optimization and their applications in computational genomics, functional genomics, and multi-omics.

  1. Graph Neural Networks: Temporal, Bipartite, and Multiplex/Multi-View Networks;
  2. Computational Genomics: Metagenomic Binning, Haplotype Phasing, and Function Prediction.

For more details, please see my CV. You can also find me on Linkedin and GitHub.
